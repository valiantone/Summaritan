OUR TECHNOLOGY

The purpose behind the construction of this tool is to form the basis of an easy to maintain online news-story reporting portal. The portal would seek to provide a one-stop destination for all the user's daily news requirements. The internet is a vast pool and it is not always easy to stay up-to-date despite its vast potential. The problem for every news seeker is that she can never read in-depth reports unless she knows the subject matter at hand. A news portal is an easy solution for just this problem, with properly credited information and stories, all in one place from a myriad source. This however is not as "easy" as it first appears, the task requires large amount of data mining, and examination, story building and monitoring which in-turn would call for a large man force or team running this portal. In this scenario we require a solution which automates the entire process, however not a simple algorithmic approach but rather a dynamic intelligent outlook to the task.
